Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information. The App may access certain device features (e.g., motion tracking or controllers) solely to provide core functionality. All such data is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted or stored externally.

2. How We Use Data

Any data generated while using the App is used only to support the App’s intended functionality. We do not use this data for advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect or store user data, there is no personal information to retain. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected data from you, you may request deletion at any time by contacting us via the email address below. We will promptly investigate and delete any such data. All users, regardless of location, may request data deletion free of charge.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will be effective when published.
